Iron Gate

Our restaurant offers multiple menu options nightly in our Dining Room as well as our Outdoor Patio and Garden. The seating for reservations is on a first come first serve basis for each location. Guests can choose from either an à la carte menu that has varying small plates and family style platters, a 5-course Tasting menu ($99 per person, additional $70 for beverage pairings,) or a 3-course Family Table menu ($59 per person, for groups of 2 or more.) Our menus change frequently based on the availability of seasonal & local ingredients, and we will do our best to customize it based on dietary restrictions & preferences.
